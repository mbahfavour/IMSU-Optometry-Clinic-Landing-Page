about us

we take care of your visual health

We are able to provide comprehensive eye care to patients, which includes everything from routine check-ups to treatment and ongoing management of visual disease or injury. Our optometrists provide care to specialized groups of individuals, such as children or the elderly. We also counsel patients on other health issues such as smoking or obesity, and how that can affect ocular health..
